Indomie

Indomie is a brand of instant noodle produced by the Indonesian company Indofood

Ingredients

  • a pack of indomie
  • water
  • onion
  • garlic

    • Steps

    1. Heat the water to a boil, then add the noodles to 3/4 'al dente' level, then remove and dry.
    2. Saute onion and garlic
    3. Mix seasonings with noodles
